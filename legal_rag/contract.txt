# Termination Clause
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.
Immediate termination is allowed in case of breach of contract.

# Force Majeure Clause
Neither party shall be liable for delays caused by natural disasters,
government actions, or events beyond reasonable control.

# Confidentiality Clause
All proprietary information must remain confidential.
Confidentiality obligations continue even after contract termination.

# Payment Terms
Payments must be made within 30 days of invoice.
Late payments may incur a 5% penalty fee.

# Dispute Resolution
All disputes shall first be resolved through mediation.
If unresolved, arbitration will take place in Chennai.

# Intellectual Property
All developed software remains property of the company.
Clients receive usage rights but not ownership.
