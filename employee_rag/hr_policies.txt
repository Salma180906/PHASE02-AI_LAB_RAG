# Remote Work Policy 2026

Manager approval is required for additional remote days.

# Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to 20 paid leaves per year.
Unused leaves can be carried forward up to 5 days.
Sick leave requires medical documentation if exceeding 2 days.
Emergency leave can be requested through HR portal.

# Work Hours
Standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
Flexible timing is allowed between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM start.
Overtime must be approved by the reporting manager.
Employees must complete 40 working hours per week.

# Code of Conduct
Employees must maintain professional behavior.
Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited.
Discrimination based on gender, religion, or race is not allowed.
Violations may result in disciplinary action.

# IT and Security Policy
Employees must raise IT tickets via the internal portal.
Company devices must not be used for personal commercial activities.
Passwords must be changed every 90 days.
Sharing login credentials is strictly prohibited.

# Performance Review Policy
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year.
Promotions depend on performance rating and business needs.
Employees scoring below expectations will be placed under review.
